Short Bio

I am a graduate student in Computer Science at the College of William and Mary (WM). My research interests include all aspects of computer architecture, including data-parallel architectures (e.g., GPUs), CPU-GPU heterogeneous architectures, and interconnection networks. I am a research assistant in the Insight Computer Architecture Lab advised by Assistant Professor Adwait Jog.
